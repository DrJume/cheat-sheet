## Download

Download a playlist as .m4a files.

```shell
youtube-dl.exe -f m4a --add-metadata -o "%(title)s.%(ext)s" https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLIeMGRbbjabyKx_z0yWcjLCNCgVpDGtFW
```
